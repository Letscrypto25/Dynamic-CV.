About Dynamic CV

Our Story

Founded in 2020 at the height of the pandemic, Dynamic CV was born from necessity - because even viruses can't stop careers! We started when two sisters, Erika and Danika, noticed how many talented professionals were struggling to showcase their skills in a rapidly changing job market.

Through lockdowns, remote work, and endless Zoom calls, we've helped over 3,000 clients land their dream jobs. Our secret? We treat every CV like it's our own - with care, expertise, and a touch of South African flair.
